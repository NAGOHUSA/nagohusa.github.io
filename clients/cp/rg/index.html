Artist Statement

Beauty
as
Direction

"Art, like the flower's sensuous detail, is extra — a North Star guiding our making; an ought."

The extra things around me — earrings, architectural trim, kitsch, pottery, and toys — greatly influence these intentionally colorful, bold in shape, rich in pattern artifacts: a bouquet of attempts at doing 'good' via art.

Several years ago, while working as a rural mailman, I came upon a flowering plant that had a quality rising above mere survival and into another realm entirely. It was beautiful. Art shares that same unnecessary extra-ness — and beauty points the way.

— Robert Grissett

About the Artist

Robert
Grissett

An artist from Southwest Georgia living in Americus with his wife and four children. His journey began with flags, then graffiti, screen printing, and finally art school.

He has shown work at local, regional, and national exhibitions from Georgia to New York.
